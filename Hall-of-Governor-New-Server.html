Overview — What is Hall of Governor?

Hall of Governor (HoG) is the most important multi-day event in Kingshot, especially on a new server. Each day focuses on a different point source. You must manage your resources carefully to maximize points on the right day.

Schedule

Every 2 weeks

Leaderboard ≠ Milestone! To earn Amadeus hero shards, you MUST finish in the Top 10 overall — completing milestones alone is not enough.

Daily Breakdown — Step by Step

Day 1

🏗️ City Construction & Research

+45 pts per Power Point from buildings & research

Complete all building and research upgrades you queued beforehand. Power gained from construction and research is converted directly into event points.

Queue major builds (Castle, Barracks, Research Center) before HoG — finish them today using speedups.

Request Alliance Help on every build — each assist cuts time = free points.

Prioritize upgrades that give the largest Power jumps (high Castle levels, top-tier Military Research).

Don't complete major upgrades BEFORE Day 1 starts — those points are permanently lost.

F2P Tip: Hoard all 5m, 15m, 60m construction speedups from daily quests and alliance chests during the week before HoG. They go a long way today.

Day 2

🦸 Hero Development

~90,000 pts per Roulette spin · Ascending heroes = big points

This is Roulette day. Each hero Roulette spin gives ~90,000 pts. Ascending heroes with shards also scores big — Rare, Epic, and Mythic give progressively higher values.

Day 4

🐉 Beast Slay & Gathering

~90,000 pts per Terror Rally · Gathering also counts

If a Beast Slay event overlaps with HoG Day 4 — double-dip both rankings simultaneously!

Day 5

⚡ Power Boost — Fallback Day

All power sources count · Low efficiency

Power Boost is the weakest scoring day. Use it only to close the gap on a milestone tier you are about to miss — never rely on it to climb the leaderboard.

Use it only if you are just short of a milestone tier worth reaching.

All power sources count: buildings, research, troops, hero upgrades — anything that adds Power.

Don't burn premium resources here if your leaderboard position can no longer change.

🆓 F2P Strategy — Free to Play

Realistic F2P target: Hit all 4 milestone tiers every day. Top 10 leaderboard in HoG 1 is very hard without spending, but milestones alone deliver enormous value.

F2P Preparation Checklist Before HoG

Collect Construction Speedups (5m, 15m, 60m) from Daily Quests, Alliance Store, and free chests — save them all for Day 1.

Collect Hero Shards & Recruitment Scrolls — do not redeem any of them until Day 2.

Collect Training Speedups from every possible source — save them for Day 3. This is your single most important investment.

Collect Stamina Bottles — do not hunt any beasts at all before Day 4.

Research Training Speed & Capacity in the military tree before HoG starts to maximize your queue size.

Queue major upgrades (Castle, Barracks, Research Center) a few hours before Day 1 so they are ready to finish with speedups.

Join an active alliance — request building help every day to save thousands of speedups.

If a big whale on your server is clearly unreachable in HoG 1, skip the rank chase and focus entirely on milestones. Save your energy for the next HoG when you are stronger.

⚙️ Governor Gear — Tips from HoG 2 Onward

Starting from HoG 2, upgrading Governor Gear contributes significant points. Each rarity or star increase grants a "max score" increment of 500 pts.

Save Forge Hammers and Enhancement Materials to use on the Gear Day of HoG.

Enhancement refunds 100% — if you want to min-max points, plan around enhancement cycles, not reforging.

Reforging Mastery refunds ~50% — use only as a last resort, it is far less efficient than enhancement.

Don't upgrade gear outside of HoG — save all materials for the Gear Day.

🗺️ Step-by-Step Timeline — New Server

H1

Day 1 — Server Opens

Join the strongest alliance. Start upgrading your Castle and core buildings. Do not burn big speedups yet. Gather resources from quests and resource tiles. Begin researching Training Speed & Capacity in the military tree.

H2-4

Days 2–4: Stockpile Phase

Hoard all speedups from daily quests, events, and alliance chests. Save hero shards. Save stamina bottles. Queue troops for training later. Do not spin the Roulette. Note the HoG countdown that appears — it fires on Day 6.

H5

Day 5 — Final Prep (Night Before HoG)

Queue major upgrades (Castle, Barracks) tonight so they finish with speedups on Day 1. Queue as many T8–T10 troops as possible for Day 3. Send gathering marches tonight to return points on Day 4. Buy a Training Capacity Buff.

H6

🏛️ HoG BEGINS! — Day 1: Construction

Immediately finish all queued building upgrades with speedups. Request Alliance Help on everything. Complete queued research. Chase all 4 milestone tiers today.

H7

Day 2: Hero Development

Redeem all saved hero shards and ascend. Spin every Roulette ticket you collected. Each spin = ~90,000 pts. This is the moment. Chase all 4 milestone tiers.

H8

⚔️ Day 3: TROOP TRAINING — Push Hard!

Burn all training speedups. Finish the troop queue you prepared. If P2W, this is the best time to buy training packs. Watch the leaderboard. Release your completed queue in the final hour as a surprise.

H9

Day 4: Beast Slay & Gathering

Burn all stamina bottles. Hunt beasts and join as many Terror Rallies as possible. Collect your gathering marches sent the night before. Coordinate with your alliance for back-to-back big rallies.

H10

Day 5: Power Boost — Final Push

Use only if you are short of a milestone. All power sources count. If you have hit all milestones and your leaderboard position is safe, rest and save resources for the next HoG.

✅ Always Do

Monitor the leaderboard daily and adjust your resource strategy based on your current position.

Join a large, active alliance — coordinated Beast Rallies are hugely beneficial on Day 4.

Set calendar reminders for each day change in HoG so you never miss the peak scoring window.

Spread resources intelligently — aim for all 4 milestone tiers every day rather than dumping everything on one day.

Exploit double-dip events — if Beast Slay overlaps with HoG Day 4, maximize both rankings at once.

❌ Never Do

🌟 Why Is Amadeus So Important?

Amadeus is the strongest rally hero in Generation 1–3. He is not just another hero — he is the defining factor in who dominates the server from the very beginning.

Rally Leader

Massive stat boost for all rally metrics — dominant in KvK and war scenarios.

Exclusive

Only obtainable from HoG Top 10 or VIP Packs 7–12.

Durability

Remains relevant even in Gen 4 — holds his own against Eric Mythic from Gen 3.

3 Chances

Available in HoG 1, 2, and 3 — three total opportunities to earn his shards.

Amadeus cannot be taken to Mythic with standard pieces — he requires exclusive pieces from VIP packs. However, the shards earned from HoG are still critically valuable for unlocking him.

🧠 Pro Tips — Advanced Strategy

Track Rivals in Real Time

Check the leaderboard every hour on Day 3. If you are safely inside Top 10 with a comfortable gap, stop spending. If you are barely outside, push harder with extra training speedups.

Surprise Tactic — Release Troops in the Final Hour

Queue a large training batch but do not complete it until the last hour of Day 3. This prevents rivals from seeing your true point total and counter-pushing before the event ends.

Coordinate Alliance Beast Rallies

One Terror Rally = ~90,000 pts. Coordinate with your alliance leader to host back-to-back big rallies on Day 4. One hour of organized play can deliver millions of points across the whole alliance.

Promote Low-Tier Troops to Higher Tiers

You earn points equal to the power difference between the old and new tier. If you have large stocks of T6/T7, promote them to T8+ on Day 3 to gain those upgrade points on top of any fresh training.

Skip the Leaderboard If Your Server Is Too Competitive

If HoG 1 is dominated by heavy spenders who are clearly out of reach, skip the leaderboard and focus entirely on milestones. Save your resources for HoG 2 when you are stronger. Milestones alone still deliver exceptional value.
