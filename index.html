// Why This Site Was Built

The Reason Behind
This Knowledge Base

Not just a tip sheet — a living archive so no knowledge gets lost and no member gets left behind.

// 01 · Problem

Information Scattered Everywhere

Tips and strategies lived in group chats, private DMs, and the memory of senior players. When new members joined or events fired, not everyone was on the same page — causing repeated mistakes and avoidable losses.

// 02 · Solution

One Place. Everything You Need.

This site is the official knowledge hub of the alliance. Every event guide, game mechanic, and formation strategy is neatly organised — accessible anytime, on any device, by anyone in the alliance.

// 03 · Standard

Raising the Whole Alliance Together

When every member understands the mechanics correctly — why Lethality beats Attack in Bear Trap, or the optimal troop split per generation — the alliance's overall performance rises significantly.

// 04 · Legacy

Knowledge That Outlasts Any Player

Players come and go — but documented knowledge stays. This site ensures the hard work and research from veterans isn't lost, but passed on to the next generation of members.
