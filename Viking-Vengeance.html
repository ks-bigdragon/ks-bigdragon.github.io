How Points Work

This is the most misunderstood mechanic. Most players think reinforcing steals points — it does not. Here's the truth:

Defense Points

Goes to: City Owner

  • Fixed reward for surviving each wave
  • Amount does not change based on who kills the Vikings
  • You receive full points even if your reinforcers do all the killing

Kill Points

Goes to: Reinforcer

  • Bonus points on top of Defense Points
  • Based on kills made inside the target city
  • The more Vikings your troops kill, the more you earn

Key Takeaway

The city owner gets full Defense Points automatically. The reinforcer earns separate Kill Points on top of that. Both players win at the same time. This is exactly why emptying your city is the best strategy — your reinforcers kill more Vikings, earn more Kill Points, and you still receive your full Defense Points regardless.

Why troops at home are a problem: If you have Infantry or Cavalry at home, they will compete with your reinforcers for kills. This directly reduces how many kills your reinforcers can score, lowering their Kill Points. An empty city = maximum kills for your reinforcers = everyone earns more.

Before the Event Starts

Must Do

  • Place your 3 best defense heroes in the Guard Station
  • Send all Infantry & Cavalry out to reinforce allies
  • Share your coordinates in Alliance chat
  • Confirm HQ captain for Wave 10 & 20
  • Stay online the full 40 minutes

Never Do

  • Heal troops during the event
  • Use a Shield — doesn't work here
  • Extinguish fires after the 1st burn
  • Leave troops gathering if going offline
  • Put defense heroes in Barricades instead of Guard Station

Troop Strategy

The goal is to maximize the kill count inside your target's city. More kills = more Kill Points. Troop type determines who gets priority inside the city's battle queue.

1

Infantry — Send first

Infantry attacks first in the battle order. Against low-health Vikings (especially in early waves), Infantry often wipes out entire rows in one turn before Cavalry even gets a chance to act.

2

Cavalry — Send second

Cavalry attacks after Infantry. In higher waves where Vikings have more health and survive Infantry's first strike, Cavalry delivers strong follow-up damage. Community testing shows Cavalry often scores more kills than expected in mid-to-late waves.

3

Archers — Leave at home

Archers fire from range and rarely score kills unless Vikings survive Infantry + Cavalry volleys. If your marches are full, leave Archers behind — they won't contribute meaningfully as reinforcers, and leaving them home doesn't significantly steal kills from your own reinforcers.

No specific ratio needed. Just hit the Balance button to auto-fill your marches. If your total troops exceed your march capacity, manually prioritize Infantry then Cavalry until all slots are full. Leave Archers behind last.

Wave-by-Wave Action Guide

Normal Waves — 1–6, 8–9, 11–13, 15–16, 18–19

  • Send all 6 march queues to reinforce allies as early as possible
  • Prioritize online players who have emptied their cities — they give your troops the cleanest kill slate
  • Avoid reinforcing offline players — their home troops compete for kills and reduce your points
  • Leave only Archers and your Guard Station heroes at home

⚠ Elite Waves — Online Only (7, 14, 17)

  • Before the wave starts: check that your target is currently online
  • Vikings march noticeably slower in these waves — use that extra time to switch targets if needed
  • If your target logs off mid-wave, immediately recall and redirect to an active online player
  • Reinforcing an offline player during Elite Waves earns zero points — their city is skipped

🔴 HQ Waves — 10 and 20

  1. 1 Wait for Wave 9 (or 19) to fully resolve — don't recall early or you lose reinforcement points
  2. 2 Recall your 1 strongest march (best heroes + highest troop count)
  3. 3 Send that march to reinforce the Alliance HQ (Plains HQ preferred)
  4. 4 Wait for Wave 10 (or 20) to fully resolve at the HQ
  5. 5 Recall from HQ → immediately send back out to reinforce a city for Wave 11+

HQ Captain: must be the player with the highest rally capacity in the Alliance

Troop type: Infantry and Cavalry only — no Archers at HQ

Critical: recalling before the wave resolves means zero points for that entire wave

Critical Rules

1. Never Heal Troops During the Event

When troops finish healing in the Hospital, they teleport back to your city instantly — even mid-wave. Once back home, those troops join the battle and steal kills from the players reinforcing you. This directly reduces your reinforcers' Kill Points score.

Safe window: You may heal between waves — but only if you can immediately send those troops back out before the next wave starts. If you can't guarantee that timing, skip the heal entirely.

2. Do Not Extinguish Fires After the 1st Burn

Your city is only completely eliminated from the event after being burned twice. After the first burn, you can still receive reinforcements and continue earning Defense Points every wave. Extinguishing fires costs Diamonds and provides zero strategic benefit during the event. Save those Diamonds — put out fires after the event ends.

3. If You Cannot Attend the Event

  • Recall all troops to your city before the event starts
  • Do not leave troops gathering or reinforcing at event time — they'll be away when Vikings attack and your city will fall instantly
  • Vikings will skip you on Elite Waves 7, 14, 17 — you only get attacked on normal waves
  • Best move: reinforce alliance members right before logging off so your march contributes even while you're gone

4. When Your City is Burned Twice

You're not out of the event — you're free. Vikings no longer target your city at all, which actually means you can go full offense:

  • Recall all remaining troops
  • Send all 6 marches immediately to reinforce other players
  • You keep earning Kill Points for every wave for the rest of the event
  • Some of the best scores come from players who got burned early and spent the whole event reinforcing
